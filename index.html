Tutoring

I've always loved teaching and, apart from working as a tutor, I've been involved in different projects regarding public education in my country. A more recent problem regarding education which arised from the lockdown is that most of schools in Peru don't have access to internet and institutions don't provide online classes. I entered a program called "Enséñame" to teach children in the meanwhile so they don't fall behind and lose the year.
